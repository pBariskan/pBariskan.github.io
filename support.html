Mergy Support

Need help with Mergy?

Mergy is a number-merging puzzle game where you drop tiles into a compact board, match equal numbers, and build chain reactions for higher scores.

Common questions

If your saved game or best score is missing, make sure the app has not been removed from the device. Mergy stores game progress locally on your device.

If audio is silent, check your device volume and silent mode, then restart the app.

Contact

For support requests, bug reports, or feedback, please open an issue on the public support repository.
