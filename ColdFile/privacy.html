Privacy Policy

Cold File is designed for offline-friendly play without accounts, ads, analytics, or cloud save.

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Overview

Cold File is designed to be played without an account, login, advertising profile, analytics account, or external game server.

Data We Collect

The game itself does not collect personal data from players. It does not use ads, third-party analytics, account creation, cloud save, or social login.

Local Game Data

Game progress, settings, rewards, and related gameplay state are stored locally on your device. This information is used only to run the game experience on that device. If you delete the app, local progress may be deleted as well.

Support Requests

If you contact support by email, we may receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, device model, iOS version, screenshots, and a description of the issue. This information is used only to respond to your request and troubleshoot the problem.

Third-Party Services

The app is distributed through Apple platforms. Apple may process information according to its own privacy policies when you download or use apps through the App Store.

This website is hosted with GitHub Pages. GitHub may process visitor information according to GitHub's own privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

Cold File is not directed at children under 13, and the game does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact poyraz@bariskan.com.
