Mergy Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Mergy is designed as a simple offline puzzle game. The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information from players.

Information Mergy stores

Mergy may store gameplay information locally on your device, such as your best score, saved game state, and whether the tutorial has been seen. This information stays on your device and is not sent to the developer.

Analytics, ads, and tracking

Mergy does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking features. The app does not access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or precise location.

Support requests

If you contact support through GitHub, any information you choose to provide in an issue or message will be handled by GitHub according to GitHub's own policies. Please avoid sharing sensitive personal information in public support requests.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when Mergy changes. The latest version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/pBariskan/pBariskan.github.io .
