MergeBeat Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

MergeBeat is designed as a simple offline puzzle game. The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information from players.

Information MergeBeat stores

MergeBeat may store gameplay information locally on your device, such as your best score, saved game state, and whether the tutorial has been seen. This information stays on your device and is not sent to the developer.

Analytics, ads, and tracking

MergeBeat does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking features. The app does not access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or precise location.

Support requests

If you contact support through GitHub or email, any information you choose to provide will be used only to respond to the request. Please avoid sharing sensitive personal information in public support requests.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when MergeBeat changes. The latest version will be posted on this page.
