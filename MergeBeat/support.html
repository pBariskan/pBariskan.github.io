MergeBeat Support

Need help with MergeBeat?

MergeBeat is a number-merging puzzle game where you drop tiles into a compact board, match equal numbers, and build chain reactions for higher scores.

Common questions

If your saved game or best score is missing, make sure the app has not been removed from the device. MergeBeat stores game progress locally on your device.

If audio is silent, check your device volume and silent mode, then restart the app.
